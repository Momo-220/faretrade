About Fare Trade Capital

Empowering investors with knowledge, confidence, and excellence since 2022.

Our Story

Fare Trade Capital is a full service real estate firm specializing in acquisition and sales of residential & commercial real estate. We develop or re-develop distressed assets, facilitating improvements through design, construction, and sales.

We work with distressed owners, short sales, REO properties, brokers, wholesale, renovations and additions. We provide a superior product at an affordable price to new homeowners, renters, and other real estate investors.

Our Mission

To empower investors with knowledge and confidence while providing superior real estate investment opportunities that benefit both our clients and the communities we serve.

Our Vision

To be the leading real estate investment firm that transforms distressed properties into thriving assets while building lasting relationships with our investor partners.
